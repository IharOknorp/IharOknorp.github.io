Frequently asked questions

Are my photos uploaded anywhere? +

Bluriva edits photos right on your device. Your images stay with you — blurring and metadata removal happen locally.

What exactly can Bluriva detect automatically? +

Faces, license plates and text are recognized automatically. You can also blur anything manually with the brush tool.

What is EXIF data and why remove it? +

EXIF is hidden information stored inside photos — including GPS coordinates, the date taken and your device model. Bluriva strips it so a shared photo can't reveal where or when it was taken.

Is Bluriva free? +

Yes. Core blurring, detection and metadata removal are free. Bluriva Pro adds batch processing and full-quality export.

Which devices are supported? +

iPhone, iPad and Mac (Apple Silicon) via the App Store, plus Android phones and tablets via Google Play.
